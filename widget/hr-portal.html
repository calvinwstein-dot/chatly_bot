HENRI Employee Handbook

Company policies, HR procedures, and employee guidelines

Effective Date: January 2026 â€¢ Internal Use Only

1. Company Overview

HENRI is a Denmark-based technology and professional services company with approximately 75 employees. The company operates with a hybrid workforce and emphasizes transparency, autonomy, and accountability.

HENRI's mission is to deliver high-quality digital solutions while maintaining a healthy, respectful, and inclusive workplace for all employees.

2. Employment Types & Contracts

Employees may be engaged as full-time, part-time, or fixed-term contractors. All employment relationships are governed by Danish employment law.

All new employees are subject to a three-month probation period. During this time, either party may terminate employment with shorter notice.

3. Working Hours & Attendance

Standard working hours: 37 hours per week, Monday to Friday

Core working hours: 09:00 to 15:00

Flexible scheduling may be agreed upon with a manager based on role requirements. Employees must track time accurately and report any absences or late arrivals to their manager promptly.

4. Compensation & Payroll

Salaries are paid monthly in arrears and deposited directly into the employee's designated Danish bank account.

Salary reviews are conducted annually based on performance and company results. Questions about compensation should be directed to HR at hr@henri.internal.

5. Leave Policies

Annual Leave

Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per year in accordance with Danish holiday legislation. Requests should be submitted in advance and approved by your manager.

Danish Public Holidays

  • New Year's Day (1 January)
  • Maundy Thursday (Thursday before Easter)
  • Good Friday (Friday before Easter)
  • Easter Monday (Monday after Easter)
  • Great Prayer Day (4th Friday after Easter)
  • Ascension Day (40 days after Easter)
  • Whit Monday (50 days after Easter)
  • Constitution Day (5 June, half day)
  • Christmas Day (25 December)
  • Second Day of Christmas (26 December)

Note: Most Danish holidays are movable and depend on Easter dates. New Year's Day, Constitution Day, and Christmas are fixed dates.

Sick Leave

Employees must notify their manager before the start of the workday. Medical documentation may be required for extended absences.

Employees may take a limited number of sick days per year to care for sick children without medical documentation. Extended child care leave requires coordination with HR.

6. Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week, subject to manager approval and business needs.

Remote Work Requirements:

  • Remain reachable during core hours (09:00-15:00)
  • Maintain productivity standards
  • Protect company data and systems
  • Ensure reliable internet connection and appropriate work environment

7. Onboarding & Offboarding

Onboarding Process

New employees participate in a structured onboarding program that includes:

  • System access setup (email, Slack, internal tools)
  • Review of company policies and procedures
  • Role-specific training
  • Introduction to team members and key stakeholders

Offboarding Process

Upon termination of employment, employees must:

  • Return all company equipment (laptop, phone, access cards, etc.)
  • Transfer knowledge and responsibilities where applicable
  • Complete exit interview coordinated by HR

8. Code of Conduct

All employees must behave professionally and respectfully.

Expected Behavior:

  • Treat colleagues with respect and professionalism
  • Act ethically and responsibly in all business dealings
  • Follow company policies and procedures
  • Maintain confidentiality of sensitive company information
  • Report any concerns or violations promptly

Zero Tolerance: Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or inappropriate behavior of any kind will not be tolerated. Contact HR immediately at hr@henri.internal to report concerns.

9. IT Systems & Data Protection

Company IT systems are provided for business use only.

Employee Responsibilities:

  • Protect login credentials and never share passwords
  • Comply with GDPR requirements for handling personal data
  • Report any suspected data breaches immediately
  • Avoid using unauthorized software or devices
  • Follow IT security best practices

10. Performance Management

HENRI conducts regular performance reviews to support employee development and align individual goals with company objectives.

Review Process:

  • Annual performance reviews with manager
  • Mid-year check-ins and ongoing feedback
  • Goal setting and development planning
  • Opportunities for training and professional growth
